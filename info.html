Information

Time

On Friday: The Main Arena will be open 2pm until midnight. The Late Night Arena will be open 12pm until 4am. On Saturday: The Main Arena will be open 11am until 2am. The Late Night Arena will be open from 8am until 4am. On Sunday: The Main Arena will be open 11am until midnight. The Late Night Arena will be open from 8am until 4am.


Location

Electric Picnic takes place in Stradbally Hall in Stradbally, Country Laois, Ireland.

Camping

General Campsite and Family Campsite will open at 9am Friday, 29th August 2019 and will close at 1pm Monday, 2nd September 2019. To view the type of camping tickets available click here.


Services

The disabled Campsite is available to those requiring assessible facilities. This campsite is located as close as possible to the arena and is staffed by the Access Team 24 hours a day throughout the event. Security and stewards are also based in the campsite.
