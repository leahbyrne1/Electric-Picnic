The Electric Picnic Story

Electric Picnic is an annual arts-and-music festival which has been staged since 2004 at Stradbally Hall in Stradbally, County Laois, Ireland. Electric Picnic began as a one-day event in 2004, before growing to a weekend-long festival within a year.
